my butterfly

i dont even know where to start anymore because every time i try to explain what you mean to me it never feels enough somehow words always feel smaller than what i actually carry for you inside my heart

somewhere between all the late conversations the random moments the laughs the silence and even the difficult days you slowly became the most important person in my life without me even realizing when it happened it felt so natural like my heart already knew you before i did

sometimes i sit there replaying tiny moments with you in my head for no reason at all something you said the way you laughed the way you looked at me or even the way your voice sounds when youre tired and somehow all those little things stay with me for hours without you even knowing

being close to you gives me a kind of peace i genuinely cannot explain its like my mind finally becomes quiet for once when im with you and honestly thats rare for me you became comfort to me in the softest way possible

i love the little things about you more than anything the things you probably dont even notice about yourself the way you react when youre excited the way you go quiet sometimes the way your presence alone changes my entire mood without even trying you became part of my everyday life so deeply that now even my thoughts naturally include you in everything

and the truth is i carry an unbelievable amount of love for you inside me sometimes it genuinely scares me because i didnt know a heart could hold this much for one person enough love to fill oceans enough to cover the entire world and somehow it still feels like my heart has more left just for you

i know im not perfect and maybe i dont always express things in the best way but every feeling i have for you is real every word every heartbeat every moment i spend thinking about you is real and i hope one day you fully understand how special you are to me because honestly i dont think anyone could ever take your place inside my heart

if life gave me the chance to choose again and again in every version of this world i genuinely think my soul would still search for yours every single time because loving you feels like the most natural thing my heart has ever done

and if im honest i think a part of me will belong to you forever no matter where life takes us no matter how much time passes there will always be a part of my heart that speaks your name softly

im always yours always
